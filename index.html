Welcome

Welcome to my Digital Teaching Portfolio, developed as part of COMP208. This portfolio is designed to showcase my growth and practice as a pre-service teacher, as well as serve as a professional resource I can continue to build on throughout my teaching career.

What to expect in this portfolio:

Within these pages you will find:

About Me: An overview of my background, studies, and journey into teaching.

Teaching Philosophy: My beliefs and values that shape my teaching pedagogy.

University Projects: Highlights of my tertiary projects that demonstrate my skills in design, technology and pedagogy.

Unit Plans and Work Samples: Examples of planning, resources, and student-focused work.

Resume: A page with my resume attached for reference.

Contact: A spot with information on how you can reach out to ask questions or collaborate.
